¡Hola, me llamo Angie! I have an advice for you!

"Confusion is your friend!"

You often learn more when you've had to work harder to get there. My struggle with a broken image ref and a night of frustration now means I can insert images and links like nobody's business.

Why? Because my mistake was simply a typo but I spent hours learning every which way to properly work with images

Part II of my advice is to always take breaks! I went to sleep frustrated, went back into troubleshooting mode the next night — my mistake was immediately clear. I had a typo in my image name that I couldn't see for staring at the same line of code all night. Stepping away from your work allows you to return later with fresh eyes.


Bonus: always remember that pizza covers all the food groups and will always cure what ails you.
